# Synthetic HR Hiring Pipeline & Workforce Reporting Dashboard

## Overview
This project uses synthetic HR hiring data to analyze applicant flow, hiring-stage bottlenecks, time-to-fill, and selection trends.

## Business Problem
HR leadership needs a repeatable view of hiring pipeline performance to identify bottlenecks, long-running requisitions, and conversion rate issues.

## Dataset
The dataset is fully synthetic and does not contain real applicant, employee, or agency data.

## Suggested Dashboard Visuals
1. Hiring funnel: Applicants to Qualified to Interviewed to Selected
2. KPI cards: Total Applicants, Total Selections, Average Time to Fill, Selection Rate
3. Bar chart: Average Time to Fill by Office
4. Bar chart: Requisitions over 90 days by Office
5. Trend chart: Hiring volume by Fiscal Year
6. Table: Long-running requisitions

## Key Findings to Highlight
1. Some offices have higher average time-to-fill than others.
2. Certain job series have lower interview-to-selection conversion.
3. Several requisitions exceed the 90-day target.
4. Management and program analyst roles have strong applicant volume.

## Recommendations
1. Review requisitions over 90 days.
2. Standardize hiring-stage tracking.
3. Monitor conversion rates by job series and office.
4. Create recurring leadership reports for pipeline health.
