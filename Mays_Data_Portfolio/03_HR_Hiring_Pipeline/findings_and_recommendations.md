# Findings and Recommendations

## Key Findings
1. Time-to-fill varies across offices.
2. Some job series have stronger applicant pools but lower final selection rates.
3. Several requisitions exceed a 90-day target.
4. Hiring-stage tracking creates opportunities for improved reporting consistency.

## Recommendations
1. Track long-running requisitions weekly.
2. Add a standard hiring-stage data dictionary.
3. Monitor conversion rates by office and job series.
4. Create recurring monthly hiring pipeline dashboards.
