# Findings and Recommendations

## Key Findings
1. Ticket volume is concentrated in several recurring categories.
2. Some support teams have higher average days open.
3. Older open tickets create SLA and customer-service risk.
4. SLA misses are more common on high-priority or escalated tickets.

## Recommendations
1. Monitor aging tickets weekly.
2. Create automated escalation rules.
3. Standardize ticket categorization.
4. Track SLA performance by team and priority.
