# Executive Briefing Pack: Slide Outline

## Slide 1: Executive Summary
- Purpose: Review housing utilization, vacancy, and data quality risks.
- Bottom Line: Underutilized buildings and inconsistent data quality require recurring review.

## Slide 2: Key Metrics
- Total Beds
- Occupied Beds
- Vacant Beds
- Average Net Use %
- Buildings Below 70% Utilization
- Open Work Orders

## Slide 3: Main Findings
- Vacancy is concentrated by installation and building type.
- Lower condition scores are associated with weaker utilization.
- Training housing shows seasonal movement.

## Slide 4: Risks
- Data quality issues can distort leadership decisions.
- Inconsistent naming reduces reporting reliability.
- Underutilized buildings may create maintenance inefficiencies.

## Slide 5: Recommendations
- Create a monthly exception report.
- Standardize installation/building names and category codes.
- Prioritize buildings with high vacancy, low condition score, and high work orders.
- Run SQL data quality checks before dashboard refresh.
